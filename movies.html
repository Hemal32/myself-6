Top anime movies of all time, according to IMDb ratings

Top anime movies of all time

Grave of the Fireflies (1988): IMDb rating: 8.5

Grave of the Fireflies portrays the harsh realities of war through the eyes of two siblings, Seita and Setsuko. This movie is a powerful testament to the resilience of the human spirit in the face of adversity, making it an unforgettable cinematic experience

Your Name (2016): IMDb rating: 8.4

Your Name is a beautifully crafted film that transcends the conventional boundaries of anime. This romantic fantasy drama follows the lives of two high school students, Taki and Mitsuha, who live in different parts of Japan but mysteriously swap bodies.

Princess Mononoke (1997): IMDb rating: 8.4

Princess Mononoke is a gripping tale of conflict between the gods of a forest and the humans who consume its resources. The film showcases Hayao Miyazaki's exceptional storytelling and his profound respect for nature. Its deep, layered narratives combined with stunning animation have made it a favorite among anime enthusiasts.
